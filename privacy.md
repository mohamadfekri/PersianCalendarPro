# Privacy Policy

## Persian Calendar Pro

Last Updated: July 2026

Persian Calendar Pro does not collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal or business data.

The visual operates entirely within Microsoft Power BI and only uses the data provided by the report.

No information is sent to external servers.

No cookies, tracking technologies, analytics services, or advertising services are used.

The developer does not have access to any customer data displayed in reports.

If future versions introduce online services, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
